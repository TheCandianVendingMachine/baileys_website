i wonder what it's like
to be a package
on a conveyer line

originally destined to go anywhere
but all possibilites allowed

does it hope that it will be
destined to go up
and be delivered by truck?

does it hope that it will be
dropped below
and flown
free for a thousand miles?

does it get worried
as the other packages
get less frequent

and its paths reduced
until the only path left

does it hope that
the noise it hears
and the teeth it sees
isn't its doom?

Written May 10th, 2025.
